About Glint Exim

Welcome to Glint Exim

With over 25 years of excellence in international trade, Glint Exim has established itself as a trusted name in import and export business.

We specialize in sourcing and supplying premium quality gemstones, exquisite jewellery, luxurious furniture, and authentic Indian spices to clients worldwide. Our commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction has helped us build long-lasting relationships with partners across the globe.

At Glint Exim, we understand the importance of quality and timely delivery in today's competitive international business environment. Our experienced team ensures that every product meets the highest standards and reaches you on time, every time.
