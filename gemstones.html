Gemstone Properties & Benefits

Discover the Power, Meaning & Healing Properties of Natural Gemstones

Ruby

The Stone of Kings

Symbol of Wealth & Power

Ruby is believed to promote loving, nurturing, health, knowledge and wealth. It has been associated with improved energy and concentration, creativity, loyalty, honor and compassion.

Properties & Benefits:

  • Promotes loving, nurturing & health
  • Improves energy and concentration
  • Enhances creativity, loyalty & honor
  • Brings good fortune & prosperity
  • Protective of home, possessions & family
  • Symbol of passion and vitality

Chinese Legend:

According to Chinese legend, the ruby was believed to be a symbol of wealth and prosperity. It was also thought to have the power to bring good fortune and ward off evil.

Sapphire

Stone of Wisdom & Royalty

Symbol of Truth & Nobility

Sapphire is known as the stone of wisdom, bringing serenity and purity to the mind. It focuses and calms the mind, releasing unwanted thoughts.

Properties & Benefits:

  • Promotes wisdom and mental clarity
  • Brings peace and serenity to the mind
  • Enhances intuition and spiritual insight
  • Supports focus and discipline
  • Associated with royalty and divine favor

Properties & Benefits:

  • Enhances unity and domestic bliss
  • Encourages growth and renewal
  • Opens the heart chakra
  • Brings harmony to relationships

Aquamarine

Stone of Courage

Calming & Protective

Aquamarine is a stone of courage. Its calming energies reduce stress and quiet the mind. It has an affinity with sensitive people and promotes self-expression.
